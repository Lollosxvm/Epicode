Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries and brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

Image : Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (5500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup entra virgin oil, plus more for the pan
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the toppings:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 1-2 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not
    necessary)
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle
attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour
is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough,
though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by
hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
